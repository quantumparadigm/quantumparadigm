1, 1 Bedroom and Flex & 2 Bedroom Apartments

The perfect balance of lifestyle, location and community

Marquee strikes the perfect balance of lifestyle, location and community. Living at Marquee not only allows you to live in a purpose built apartment, it allows you to live on 16th Avenue. Close to downtown, diverse restaurants, fitness uses, grocery stores and parks. We’ve designed everything with you in mind.

Have peace of mind knowing that you live in a safe and secure building that has a state of the art security system and onsite management. We want you to have the opportunity to know your neighbours and neighbourhood so we’ve invested in an interactive website that will provide you with access to building forums. We will also be hosting many building events.

It’s a shame to live in a city like Calgary, ranking in the top 10 sunniest cities in Canada, and not have a patio to take advantage of the warmer days and the city and mountain views. Say no more, we’ve created two patios on the second and seventh floor so you can have extra space to call home and to socialize with your neighbours and friends while taking in the sights.

Living in such an accessible community makes it easy to live a car free lifestyle, but sometimes you may need to get on the highway and drive out to the mountain or across town for an appointment which is why we offer underground parking. We also offer out of suite bike storage to keep your suite clutter free and to make that lifestyle choice much easier.

At Marquee on 16th we believe in hospitality. Living in a rental should be easier and more pleasant than living in a purchased unit. It’s this philosophy that led to on site management offices and resident maintenance superintendents. We strive to provide you with a maintenance free lifestyle.
